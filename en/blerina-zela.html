Blerina Zela

Share

https://durresbiennale.org/en/blerina-zela

Blerina ZELA is a professional translator and photographer. She graduated in English at the “Aleksandër Xhuvani” State University in Elbasan in 2012. Then in 2014, at the same university, she defended her Master's degree in English, teacher profile. While in 2018 she successfully defended the title of Master of Science in literary translation techniques and interpretation in English at the State University of Tirana.

In addition to undergraduate and postgraduate studies in English, she is fluent in Italian, Spanish and Japanese. As a photographer, she took part in several professional trainings, which she turned into her current profession alongside translation.
