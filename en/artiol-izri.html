Artiol Izri

Share

https://durresbiennale.org/en/artiol-izri

Artiol IZRI was born in Durrës. He is an artist and illustrator, who has shown a special passion for painting since childhood. In 2019, he graduated in easel painting at the “Naim Frasheri” Artistic High School in Vlora, specializing in scenography. The years of studying painting at the artistic high school of Vlora in the first steps for his professional engagement in the field of visual art.

In 2019, he moved to study at the Florence Academy of Fine Arts in Italy, where he graduated in painting in 2022 and is currently pursuing postgraduate studies in illustration.

Throughout his artistic career, he has exhibited in many national and international exhibitions in Albania, Italy and elsewhere. These experiences have helped him, not only in his artistic growth, but also in the recognition of his creativity at professional levels.

Artiol develops his artistic activity between Florence and Albania, transforming this interweaving of academic training, practical experience and dedication to visual art and illustration into a stimulating relationship full of opportunities for the development of his artistic career. This approach makes him determined to continue his growth and formation as a professional in the field of art and illustration, contributing his creativity and experience to the “landscape” of contemporary Albanian art.
