Xhenisa Shahini

Share

https://durresbiennale.org/en/xhenisa-shahini

Xhenisa SHAHINI is a lawyer and translator. She graduated in law at “Aleksandër Moisiu” University in Durrës. He also defended his Master of Science degree in international relations at the Mediterranean University of Tirana. Her entire formative journey has been enriched with several trainings such as: the development of foreign languages of Anglo-Saxon origin, part of the European Movement Albania for the “Europe for Citizens” program in Brussels and the development of computer skills in various programs such as; Graphic design, Digital Marketing and SEO, Windows Movie Maker and Office.

Following her studies in the field of foreign languages, she has an excellent command of several languages: English, Italian, French, German and Spanish.

In addition to her university studies, Xhenisa has participated in two important scientific conferences, such as: the national conference on Reform in Law and the national conference on the Integration of the Republic of Albania in the European Union, held at the “Aleksandër Moisiu” University in Durrës.
