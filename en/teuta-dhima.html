Teuta Dhima

Share

https://durresbiennale.org/en/teuta-dhima

Teuta DHIMA was born in 1975 in Vlora. After graduating from the Pedagogical High School “November 17”, she began her studies at the State University of Tirana, Faculty of Philology and History, branch of Albanian language and literature, which were followed by those at the National School of Librarianship and the 1-year specialization course, in The National Library of Albania.

Since 2007, she has been working as a bibliographer at the Durrës Public Library, where she moderates meetings dedicated to the book that she develops. She organizes and moderates since 2023, the meetings of the “Gjergj Vlashi” Book Club.

Her publishing activity has brought cooperation with the newspapers: “Dyrrah”, “Sintagma”, “Fjala (Word)”, “Nacional”, “Liberale”, “ExLibris”, where she has published reviews, articles, reports, essays and analyses.

While her activity in publishing includes the editing and proofreading of poetry books and novels, for the “2M” and “Jozef” publishing houses.

She is the author of two books of poetry: “Beg the earth” (2021) and “Why is a sunflower thought of” (2023).
